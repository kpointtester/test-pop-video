Our Story

KPOINT is a video technology company transforming business communications with interactive videos. We recognize video is the primary medium of future communication over the internet and innovate to bring the best of the two worlds — videos and web — together to make business communication effective and conversational. Our focus is on positively impacting business outcomes rather than mere consumption of content.

Unlike consumer media, business communication needs to be time-efficient with a razor sharp focus on the ability to deliver the desired message quickly and in the most engaging fashion. Business audiences loathe to consume content linearly. Our videos make sure users have the right aids to consume video their way in a non-linear fashion.

Our streamlined approach to content enables us to address specific business needs across customer touchpoints ensuring measurable impact:
